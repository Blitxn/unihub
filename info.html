Available Scholarships

Full Scholarship

Government Merit Scholarship

Awarded to top-performing students based on national exam results. Covers full tuition for the entire degree program.

📅 Deadline: August 30, 2025 🎓 Undergraduate
Partial Scholarship

RUPP Excellence Award

For students with GPA above 3.5. Covers 50% of tuition fees per semester with renewal every year.

📅 Deadline: September 15, 2025 🎓 All levels
Full Scholarship

Japanese Government (MEXT)

Bilateral scholarship for Cambodian students. Includes tuition, living allowance, and airfare to Japan for graduate studies.

📅 Deadline: October 1, 2025 🎓 Postgraduate
Partial Scholarship

Need-Based Financial Aid

Supports students from low-income families. Covers up to 75% of tuition based on family income assessment.

📅 Deadline: July 20, 2025 🎓 Undergraduate
